Playing with Firefox OS

After acquiring a free ZTE Open from Movistar (Well not actually for free, we know that movistar always in somehow you're going to be cheated) was nothing more than a matter of time that sting me the itch to this OS. The first thing I proposed it was compile the latest version 2.0 at that time, since the Movistar buddies upgrades never go futher than 1.1 version... The truth is that the reference pages for Firefox are pretty good, although the process is a bit tedious and easily obtainable configuration errors. In my case, the only thing that worked for me was to create me a virtual machine on a Windoze (to avoid complications to connect your mobile by USB as it is necessary for compilation !! WTF ???) with Ubuntu 12.04 which is the environment that recommended.
